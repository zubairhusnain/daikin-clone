About Daikin

Creating air around the world for greater happiness and greater comfort—
To realize a sustainable society, Daikin will pursue new values in the environment and air.
For Perfecting the Air, Daikin will pursue solutions in the realm of air to contribute to the well-being of the “earth” “cities” and “people.”
